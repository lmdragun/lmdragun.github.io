Screenreaders

JAWS

  • Most widely used
  • PC-only
  • Costs around $1000 USD

NVDA

  • Free screen reader
  • Fairly popular after JAWS/built-in ones

Narrator

  • Comes (free) with Windows
  • Activate with Ctrl + Windows + Enter

Voice Over

  • Comes (free) with Macs
  • Activate with Command + F5
